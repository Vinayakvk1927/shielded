About us
Welcome to ShieldedSolutions, your reliable source for premium helmet rentals! Established with a passion for safety and a commitment to providing top-notch protective gear, we aim to offer convenience and peace of mind to our customers.
At ShieldedSolutions, we understand the paramount importance of safeguarding yourself while enjoying your rides. Our story began with a simple yet crucial idea: to make high-quality helmets easily accessible to everyone. Whether you're an avid biker, scooter enthusiast, or someone seeking safe headgear for various outdoor adventures, we've got you covered.
Our Services.
1. Helmet Rental
Experience convenience and affordability with our helmet rental services. Whether you're a tourist exploring the city, a weekend adventurer, or someone trying out a new hobby, we offer a diverse range of high-quality helmets for short-term and long-term rentals.
2. Helmet Fitting and Adjustment
Your safety matters to us. Our team of experts ensures that every helmet you rent fits perfectly and meets safety standards. We offer personalized fitting services to guarantee a comfortable and secure fit for your chosen helmet.
3.Helmet Maintenance and Sanitization
Your safety is our priority. We meticulously inspect and sanitize every helmet after each use, ensuring they are in pristine condition and ready for the next renter. Our maintenance procedures guarantee helmets that meet the highest safety standards.
